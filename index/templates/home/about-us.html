About us:

As a group of gamers ourselves, we are not immune to the woes and struggles that come with (either accidentally or willingly) amassing a gaming backlog of games. We at StackOverflowers Studios © created My Backlog Handler™ in order to help fellow gamers tackle this problem by and guide them in the process that is growing their collections, as well as tracking their progress in each game they have in their collection so everyone can have a complete gaming experience!
